What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that relates body weight (kg) to height (m). BMI is sometimes used to measure total body fat and whether a person is a healthy weight.

Nutrition Tips Section

  • Achieve a Balanced Diet
    • Portion Control: Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating.
    • Limit Sugar and Salt: Reduce your intake of sugary snacks and drinks, and watch out for high sodium levels in processed foods.
  • Stay Hydrated
    • Drink Plenty of Water: Aim for at least 8 glasses a day.
  • Plan and Prepare Your Meals
    • Meal Prep: Prepare meals in advance to avoid unhealthy last-minute choices.
    • Read Labels: Check nutritional labels for hidden sugars, unhealthy fats, and high sodium content.

Exercise Tips

  • Start Small: Begin daily exercises, such as walking or stretching.
  • Incorporate Cardio: Engage in cardiovascular exercises like jogging or cycling.
  • Stay Active Daily: Find ways to stay active such as doing household chores.
